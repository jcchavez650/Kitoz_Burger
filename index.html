Our Story

Big flavor, no shortcuts

At Kitoz Burger we do one thing and we do it loud — smash fresh beef thin on a screaming-hot flat-top for those crispy, caramelized edges, then stack it with the good stuff. From the over-the-top La 4x4 to the sweet-heat Happy Costa Burger, every order is made to order.

Come hungry. Leave happy. That's the whole plan.
